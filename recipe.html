S'mores Recipe


Click here for the instructional video!

Ingredients

  • graham crackers
  • chocolate
  • marshmellows
  • wooden skewer

Instructions

  1. Take two graham crackers and place them side by side.
  2. Place a piece of chocolate on one cracker
    3. Warning: Children under 10, please ask an adult for help on this steps!
  3. Skewer the marshmellow, then take the skewer by the clean end
  4. Turn on the stove and hold the skewered marshmellow over the flame
    5. TIP: for an even roast, turn the skewer slowly!
  5. Once the marshmellow is suffiecently toasted, turn the stove off
  6. Place the marshmellow on the chocolate piece
  7. Top the layered cracker with the other cracker and slowly pull the skewer out
    8. For best results, squeeze the top layer gently while removing the skewer!
  8. Wait until the marshmellow cools
    9. And Enjoy!
